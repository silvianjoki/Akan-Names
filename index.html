Akan Naming Generator

Hello and Welcome

Within this web application, we will generate your Akan name. This uses your date of birth information and gender to provide results on the name as well as the day of birth during the week.

According to Ghanian culture, children are named based on the day name (which corresponds to the day in the week they are born). By generating your Akan name, you will find out the corresponding day of the week you were born.
